Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us

Last Updated: June 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to improve your experience: email (if you subscribe), browser type, device type, and page interaction data via cookies and analytics.

2. How We Use Your Data

To personalize your gaming experience, send game updates (if subscribed), serve relevant ads via Google AdSense, and improve our website performance.

3. Cookies

We use cookies for theme preference storage, analytics (Google Analytics), and ad personalization. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google AdSense for ads, Google Analytics for traffic analysis, and Font Awesome for icons. These services may collect data as per their own privacy policies.

5. Data Security

We implement HTTPS encryption, regular security audits, and never sell your personal data to third parties.

6. Your Rights

You can request data deletion, unsubscribe from emails anytime, disable cookies, and contact us for any privacy concerns.

7. Contact Us

For privacy-related queries, email us at: privacy@gameverseindia.com
